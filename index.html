What top therapy sites have that the current site doesn’t

Today’s live site is a clean SimplePractice template. It works as a brochure + portal — but elite private practices convert more clients with brand, content, and frictionless booking.

Current site (SimplePractice)

  • Generic “Working Together” hero copy
  • One long services bullet list
  • No dedicated specialty landing pages
  • No “what to expect” new-client journey
  • No FAQ / insurance how-to
  • No blog or local SEO content
  • No testimonials / social proof block
  • No online consult form (phone/portal only)
  • Template visual system (not brand-forward)

FAQ — missing on the live site

Answers people need before they call

Do you offer telehealth?

Yes. Appointments are available both in-person in Sioux City and via telehealth when appropriate.

What ages and formats do you see?

Teens and adults — individuals, couples, and families — for mental health, behavioral, and addiction-related concerns.

How does insurance work?

Many major plans are accepted (including BCBS/Wellmark, UHC/Optum, Aetna, Cigna, and others). Coverage varies by plan — we’ll help you verify when you reach out.

What should I expect in the first session?

A collaborative conversation about what’s bringing you in, your strengths, and goals. You’ll leave with a clearer plan — not a one-size-fits-all script.

How do existing clients schedule?

Use the secure client portal for scheduling, paperwork, and online payments when enabled.

Is this an emergency service?

No. If you are in crisis or thinking about harming yourself, call 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) or 911, or go to the nearest emergency room.
